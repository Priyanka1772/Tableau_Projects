## Superstore Sales Analysis (4) - Tableau Project
## Project Summary
* To uncover key insights into customer purchasing patterns, regional sales performance, and product profitability within the Superstore dataset.
* To discover underlying trends and presence of outliers  to identify unusual transactions, potential data errors. 

## Features:
### Comprehensive Performance Overview:
* Visualizes Sales and Profit trends over time (e.g., by year, quarter, month based on Order Date) to identify seasonality and growth patterns.
* Discount Impact Analysis: Visualizes the distribution and impact of discounts across records, helping to understand their effectiveness on sales and profitability." This connects it more directly to effectiveness and outcome.
### Detailed Product & Category Insights:
* Profitability Analysis: Offers granular views into Product Category performance, clearly highlighting which products are most profitable and which might be incurring losses (e.g., your "Tables" insight).
* Analyzes Product Container types against Shipping Cost to identify unusual shipping expenses or outliers using a box plot, optimizing logistics." (Or similar, depending on the insight derived).
### Dynamic Filtering & Interactivity:
* Date Range Filters: Allows users to select specific time periods (e.g., year, quarter, or custom date range) to analyze trends within desired windows.

## Technologies Used
* Tableau Public: For data visualization, analysis and publishing.
